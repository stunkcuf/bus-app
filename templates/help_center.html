Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Click on your username in the top right corner
  2. Select "Profile" from the dropdown menu
  3. Click "Change Password"
  4. Enter your current password and new password
  5. Click "Update Password"

If you've forgotten your password, contact your administrator.

What do I do if a student is absent?

When a student is absent:

  1. Leave their attendance checkbox unchecked
  2. Don't enter a pickup time
  3. Add a note in the trip notes section if needed
  4. The system will automatically mark them as absent
How do I view my past trip logs?

To view your trip history:

  1. Go to your Driver Dashboard
  2. Scroll down to "Recent Trip Logs"
  3. For older logs, click "View Reports" in the navigation
  4. Select "Driver Reports" and choose your date range
Can I use the system on my phone?

Yes! The Fleet Management System is mobile-friendly:

  • Open your phone's web browser
  • Go to the same website address you use on computer
  • Log in with your usual credentials
  • The interface will automatically adjust for your screen

For best experience, use Chrome or Safari browsers.
