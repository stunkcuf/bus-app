Safe Learning Environment

Practice mode creates a sandbox with sample data. You can:

  • Try all features without fear
  • Make mistakes and learn from them
  • Test different scenarios
  • Understand the workflow

What's Included

Your practice environment will have:

  • 5 sample buses with maintenance records
  • 5 routes with different configurations
  • 20 students with complete information
  • 10 days of historical trip logs

Perfect For

  • New users learning the system
  • Training sessions and demonstrations
  • Testing new features before using them
  • Understanding reports and analytics

How It Works

  1. Click "Start Practice Mode" below
  2. Sample data will be generated instantly
  3. Navigate normally - a green banner shows you're in practice mode
  4. Exit anytime to return to real data

Practice mode lasts for 2 hours or until you exit

{{end}}

Practice Mode Tips

Try These Actions:
  • Log a morning route with student attendance
  • Add a new student to your roster
  • Schedule bus maintenance
  • Generate and export reports
  • Assign drivers to routes (managers)
What to Expect:
  • Green banner on all pages in practice mode
  • All features work exactly like real mode
  • Data persists during your session
  • No emails or notifications are sent
  • Can't affect other users or real data
